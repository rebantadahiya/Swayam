FAQ's

I eat so less. Only two chapattis in the morning and one at night. Still I am obese. Why?

This is a problem for many. My advice is that you please make a detail note of everything you eat and drink in a day. Then count the actual calorie intake. You will get the answer. It is not just about chapattis, but the entire calorie intake.

There are so many stresses. Is it possible to control all? I have tried methods as in some books but failed.

The right answer to manage your stress only comes after understanding its basic mechanisms and control. This programme gives you practical training to control your stresses. Books cannot be 100% followed until you have the right understanding for YOURSELF.

Is it important to take my medicines for high blood pressure daily? I only take when I feel my blood pleasure is up.

It is very important to take your high blood pressure medicines daily. If you have any doubt that your blood pressure is normal, do consult your doctor before stopping medicines. They should not be taken as on occasion schedule.
