What is Cardiac Rehabilitation and Preventive Cardiology?

Cardiac Rehabilitation is getting the heart back to its best function to recover from heart attack, other forms of heart disease or surgery by following a customized program of exercise and education designed to help improve the health of the person. Preventive Cardiology focuses on lowering a person’s risk of developing heart diseases and having a heart attack or stroke and also preventing further issues in people who already have cardiovascular disease.


Why is Stress Management important for me?

Stress is the number one killer, worldwide, since it is a major risk factor for diseases like heart disease, hypertension, diabetes, depression, anxiety, psychosomatic diseases, stomach ulcers, joint pains and so on. In today’s world almost everyone is stressed about their own set of problems and hence it becomes necessary to take control of the situation by reducing the stress by handling the root causes before it starts controlling you.
