About Us

SWAYAM provides wellness experience programs that aims to transform the way you think about your heart, its health and your lifestyle. SWAYAM combines cardiac expertise, stress management, meditation, diet and exercise elements to enable you to live a healthier, fitter life for yourself and your loved ones.

Started by Dr. Rajeev Sharma, a cardiac rehabilitation expert and a chest physician, who believes that his life is more useful if he can serve the humanity, by putting his passion, knowledge and energy to the betterment of as many individuals as possible. As a practicing physician, he focuses on the well-being of not only his patients but also every human being.

As the name suggests, SWAYAM is a self-insight program that brings the change from within you. By following the program, you stay healthy and increase life with the right scientific medical knowledge preventing modern diseases and enjoying a eustress happy life. It takes care of your routine everyday life leading to more fulfilment and achievements.

We conduct extensive workshop training for everybody where all the non-invasive know how’s of medical science to prevent heart disease and related diseases (hypertension, diabetes, obesity etc) along with stress management, diet management, exercises and meditation is taught to the participants. The combination is made in such a way that one can follow them in their life very easily. Practicality is assured in this training. This program will help all to prevent heart disease and live more of a healthy and happy life. This is a cost effective, scientifically designed medical program. Swayam is conducted for Corporate, Groups, Housing societies and Hospitals. Also, attendees can avail of consultation and counselling with Dr Rajeev Sharma post the program.

Our Vision

Our effort is for the People to understand their health, manage their stress, life and role towards oneself. SWAYAM has its roots from a simple yet a long back endeavor of being healthy, self-reliant and disease-free for life.

Our Mission


We aspire to make people independent when it comes to living an overall healthy life. People that enrol into our programs, should flourish in their well-being.
