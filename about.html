About Us

SWAYAM is a wellness experience program that aims to transform the way you think about your heart, its health and your lifestyle. SWAYAM combines cardiac expertise, stress management, meditation, diet and exercise elements to enable you to live a healthier, fitter life for yourself and your loved ones.

Started by Dr. Rajeev Sharma, a cardiac rehabilitation expert and a chest physician, who believes that his life is more if he can serve the humanity, by putting his passion, knowledge, energy, to the betterment of as many individuals as possible. As a practicing physician, he focuses on the well-being of not only his patients but every human being.

SWAYAM is based on the concepts pioneered by Dr Dean Ornish for management & reversal of cardiac disease in the 1980s. In India, the concept was pioneered by Dr BimalChajjer in Delhi, who was trained by Dr Ornish. Dr Rajeev Sharma has worked with and been mentored by Dr Chajjer, whom Dr Sharma believes to be his Guru.

He puts his faith in almighty, and repeats the old saying, “As you sow, so shall you reap”. He listens to his inner voice, which all the time tells him to work on removing the unnecessary pain and suffering.

What we do?

SWAYAM conducts 1 day or 2 day extensive workshop training for everybody where all the non-invasive know how’s of medical science to prevent heart disease and related diseases (hypertension, diabetes, obesity etc) along with stress management, diet management, exercises and meditation is taught to the participants. The combination is made in such a way that one can follow them in their life very easily. Practicality is assured in this training. This program will help all to prevent heart disease and live more of a healthy and happy life. This is a cost effective, scientifically designed medical program. Swayam is conducted for Corporate, Groups, Housing societies and Hospitals. Also, attendees can avail of consultation and counselling with Dr Rajeev Sharma post the program.

As the name suggests, SWAYAM is a self-insight program that brings the change from within you. By following the program, you stay healthy and increase life with the right scientific medical knowledge preventing modern diseases and enjoying a eustress happy life. It takes care of your routine everyday life leading to more fulfilment and achievements.

The diet modification helps you to maintain your cholesterol and triglyceride levels. It helps you to remain active and enjoy a good health for entire life. With the right knowledge, you can have all that which your tastes buds crave for, but in a healthy way. Exercise maintains your flexibility. The little pains here and there vanishes with proper ergonomics. The problems such as arthritis and spondylitis can be managed and improved.

Meditation again helps you to increase your concentration. You become more alert to what is happening around. You can analyse situations better and take better and right decisions to your and everybody’s benefit. Your productivity at work invariably increases.

We also give consultations and counselling. You can consult with Dr Rajeev Sharma for any medical problem. We help you to tackle you problems individually. We give you an entire understanding for mental, physical and spiritual health.
